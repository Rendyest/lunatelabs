What I Build

Hardware and AI for rehabilitation, built to work in a clinic, not just a demo.

Mirra Call

A computer-vision rehabilitation device that watches movement and gives real-time feedback, without a clinician standing over every rep. Runs on an NVIDIA Jetson hardware stack with MediaPipe for pose tracking and a local LLM for on-device reasoning — no patient video ever needs to leave the room.
